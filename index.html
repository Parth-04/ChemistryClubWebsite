About Us

Welcome to the Chemistry and ChemE TL Club! Our club is a dynamic community fostering a passion for chemistry and chemical engineering. Through our Chemical Engineering TL, students explore processes and engage in hands-on experiments, gaining practical insights. Beyond traditional learning, our club hosts diverse events and workshops, creating an environment where knowledge meets creativity. The "Reactorious" project series allows students to experiment on topics of interest, promoting innovation and collaboration. Join us in unlocking the mysteries of the molecular world, where curiosity and experimentation drive collective scientific growth. Explore, discover, and create with us in the ever-evolving landscape of chemistry and chemical engineering.
